Regulations

  • Based from Gt3 cars from FIA.
  • weight:2,645-2,866 pounds.
  • Horsepower:500-600.
  • Transmission must be converted to a sequential gearbox.
  • engine can be swapped or modified but must remain in its original position.
  • safety:roll cage, race spec brakes, racing seat and harnesses
  • car must have at least 300+ production units made
